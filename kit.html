Suggested Gear & Kit Guide

Ausrüstungsliste

Impression period: 1916–1918. Two entry-level impressions are supported.

rüstung

Musketier — Standard Recruit

  • Tunic (choose per portrayed year): M1907/10 or M1907/14 Feldrock (early war), or M1915 Feldbluse (mid-war) — feldgrau wool. Members may also choose to wear an older pattern tunic than their portrayed year strictly requires, since older stock remained in use throughout the war
  • Trousers (choose per portrayed year): Feldhose M1907, M1915, or M1917 pattern, feldgrau, red piping. As with tunics, wearing an older trouser pattern is always acceptable, since these remained in service alongside newer issue
  • Marschstiefel or puttees + ankle boots
  • M1916/M1918 Stahlhelm, correct liner
  • Single button, left collar corner — rank mark
  • Lederkoppel, Brotbeutel-Trageriemen (bread bag strap), Patronentaschen, Brotbeutel, Feldflasche
  • Seitengewehr, Spaten, gas mask canister (1916+)

Unteroffizier — Founder / Leadership

  • All Musketier items, plus:
  • Tresse (silver/white NCO lace), collar & cuffs
  • Shoulder straps, regimental number "29" Piping: red (1914–15) transitioning to white (1916–18)
  • Soldbuch reproduction + persona service history
